I’m a wildlife fanatic with a love of words and a penchant for graphic design. I live with my husband and our two dogs in Clayton, North Carolina.

My current job involves both writing and design work. Since starting there, I’ve learned about print production, the prepress process, large-format design, and digital design. I’ve written for print newsletters, e-newsletters, blog posts, social media, donor communications and more. I’ve designed t-shirts, banners, event invitations, newsletters, handouts, and social media graphics.
